Map Creation Overview

Terrains consist mainly of a heightmaps and assets (trees, buildings etc.).

Heightmaps

The heightmap will be the foundation for your terrain, we recommend using a procedural generator like World Machine 2, L3DT or Geo Control 2. This takes some time to get right (and if you use a generator like World Machine it is important to get it right before moving to manual editing) but is very important for the final terrain.

Assets

It is very important to have an asset list before jumping into level building. This will enable you to work your way through them one by one and help to unify the look of your level. Assets consist of texture files and meshes. These can be created with the image and 3D editor of your choice. (Blender3D, Paint.Net etc.)
